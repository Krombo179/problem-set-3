A Brief Overview

What Is Supreme?

"Supreme is an American skateboarding shop and clothing brand established in New York City in April 1994. The brand caters to the skateboarding, hip hop, and rock cultures, as well as to the youth culture in general. The brand produces clothes and accessories and also manufactures skateboards."

What Is On This Website?

This website will showcase Dustin Hartenstein's Supreme clothing collection, how much each was purchased for, and the resale value.
